Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register for an event?

Click the "Register Now" button on any event card. You'll be guided through our secure registration process. You can pay via credit card, bank transfer, or cryptocurrency.

Can I get a recording if I miss the live event?

Yes! All registered attendees receive access to event recordings and materials for 30 days after the event. Past events are available in our "Past Events" section.

Are there group discounts?

Yes, we offer 15% discount for groups of 3 or more. Contact our events team at events@taagc.com for group registration.

How do I become a speaker?

We're always looking for industry experts. Submit your speaking proposal at speakers@taagc.com with your topic and bio.
