About TAAGC Fintech

Empowering Nigerians with innovative financial solutions through strategic partnership with 9 Payment Service Bank

Who We Are

Triple A-Ahal Global Concept (TAAGC) is a dynamic Nigerian company registered with BN8514665, engaged in multiple lines of business including contract servicing, agriculture, grain processing, ICT, and general trading.

In partnership with 9 Payment Service Bank (9PSB) - Nigeria's first Payment Service Bank licensed by the CBN - we provide innovative financial technology solutions to the underbanked, unbanked, and banked populations across Nigeria.

Our fintech platform offers seamless access to airtime recharge, data bundles, utility bill payments, betting funding, virtual accounts, and agent banking services.
