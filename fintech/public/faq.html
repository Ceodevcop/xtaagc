Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

How do I create an account?
You can create an account by clicking the "Register" button on our homepage. You'll need to provide your phone number, full name, and complete OTP verification. After registration, you'll need to complete KYC verification to access all services.
What documents do I need for KYC?
You'll need a valid government ID (National ID, International Passport, or Driver's License), your BVN, and a passport photograph. For agents, additional business documents are required including CAC registration and SCUML certificate.
How long does KYC verification take?
KYC verification typically takes 24-48 hours. You'll receive an email notification once your account is verified.
How do I buy airtime?
Log in to your dashboard, select "Airtime", choose your network, enter the phone number and amount, then confirm the transaction. The airtime will be delivered instantly.
Can I pay electricity bills for any DISCO?
Yes, we support all DISCOs including AEDC, EEDC, KAEDC, PHED, JED, IBEDC, and IEDC. Simply select your DISCO, enter your meter number, and the amount to pay.
How do I fund my betting account?
Select "Betting" from your dashboard, choose your betting provider (Bet9ja, BetKing, etc.), enter your customer ID, and the amount. Funds are transferred instantly.
How do I become an agent?
To become an agent, you need to pay the ₦400,000 onboarding fee, complete the agent registration form, and provide required documents including CAC registration, TIN, SCUML certificate, and director's information. Contact us to start the process.
What is the minimum daily transaction for agents?
Agents are required to process a minimum of 30 transactions per terminal daily. Failure to meet this target may result in terminal recall.
How much commission do agents earn?
Agents earn: 0.04% on cash-out (₦25 cap), ₦4 flat on transfers, 25% on bills and airtime commissions, ₦50-₦120 per account opening, and ₦1.40 on micro-payment transactions.
Is my money safe with TAAGC Fintech?
Yes, your money is safe. We partner with 9PSB, a CBN-licensed Payment Service Bank. All funds are held in insured accounts, and we use bank-grade 256-bit encryption to protect your data.
What happens if I forget my password?
You can reset your password by clicking "Forgot Password" on the login page. An OTP will be sent to your registered phone number to verify your identity.
How do I report suspicious activity?
Report any suspicious activity immediately to our fraud hotline at 08023566143 or email dev.cxxp@gmail.com. For data breaches, contact our DPO at dpo@9psb.com.ng.
What are the transaction fees?
Fees vary by service: Airtime (3-7.71%), Bills (0.86-1.29%), Betting (0.13-0.86%), Transfers (₦10.75), and Virtual Accounts (0.5%). Stamp duty of ₦50 applies to transactions of ₦10,000 and above.
Are there any hidden charges?
No, we believe in transparent pricing. All fees are clearly displayed before you confirm any transaction. There are no hidden charges.
When are commissions paid to agents?
Commissions are earned in real-time on all transactions. Additional monthly commissions are calculated based on incremental transaction volume and paid within 5 business days of month end.
