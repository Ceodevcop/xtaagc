Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 15, 2025

1. Introduction

Triple A-Ahal Global Concept ("TAAGC", "we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our fintech platform and services, in compliance with the Nigeria Data Protection Regulation (NDPR) and other applicable laws.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

  • Full name
  • Phone number
  • Email address
  • Bank Verification Number (BVN)
  • National Identification Number (NIN)
  • Date of birth
  • Address
  • Government-issued ID (passport, driver's license, voter's card)
  • Passport photograph

2.2 Financial Information

  • Bank account details
  • Transaction history
  • Wallet balances
  • Payment card information (processed securely by 9PSB)

2.3 Technical Information

  • IP address
  • Device information
  • Browser type
  • Location data
  • Cookies and usage data

3. How We Collect Information

  • Direct Collection: When you register, complete KYC, or use our services
  • Automated Collection: Through cookies, logs, and analytics tools
  • Third Parties: From 9PSB, credit bureaus, and identity verification services

4. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain our services
  • To process transactions and payments
  • To verify your identity (KYC compliance)
  • To comply with CBN and NDPR regulations
  • To prevent fraud and enhance security
  • To communicate with you about your account
  • To improve our platform and user experience
  • To calculate and pay commissions

5. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your information based on:

  • Consent: You have given explicit consent
  • Contract: Processing is necessary for our agreement
  • Legal Obligation: Compliance with CBN, NDPR, and other laws
  • Legitimate Interests: Fraud prevention, network security

6. Information Sharing

We may share your information with:

  • 9 Payment Service Bank (9PSB): Our primary banking partner
  • Regulatory Authorities: CBN, NITDA, EFCC, when required by law
  • Service Providers: Identity verification, analytics, customer support
  • Payment Processors: For transaction processing

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

7. Data Security

We implement robust security measures including:

  • 256-bit AES encryption for data at rest
  • TLS 1.2+ for data in transit
  • Multi-factor authentication
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Access controls and least privilege principles
  • API key rotation every 90 days
  • 24/7 security monitoring
  • Incident response procedures (48-hour breach notification)

8. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active and for 5 years after account closure, as required by CBN regulations. After this period, your data will be securely deleted or anonymized.

9. Your Rights

Under NDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Rectify inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data (subject to legal requirements)
  • Restrict or object to processing
  • Data portability
  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • Lodge a complaint with NITDA

To exercise these rights, contact our Data Protection Officer at dev.cxxp@gmail.com

10. Cookies

We use cookies to enhance your experience. You can control cookie settings through your browser. Essential cookies are required for platform functionality.

11. International Data Transfers

Your information may be processed outside Nigeria where our service providers operate. We ensure such transfers comply with NDPR requirements through appropriate safeguards.

12. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for individuals under 18. We do not knowingly collect information from minors.

13. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this policy periodically. We will notify you of material changes via email or platform notification.
