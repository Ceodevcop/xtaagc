Investment Glossary

APR Annual Percentage Rate - the yearly interest rate earned on an investment
Compound Interest Interest calculated on the initial principal and accumulated interest
Diversification Spreading investments across different assets to reduce risk
ETF Exchange-Traded Fund - a type of investment fund traded on stock exchanges
Liquidity How quickly an investment can be converted to cash
ROI Return on Investment - the profit or loss generated on an investment
Volatility The degree of variation in investment prices over time
