Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about investing with TAAGC Global

General Questions

What is TAAGC Global?

TAAGC Global is a premier investment platform that connects investors with carefully vetted opportunities worldwide. We provide access to institutional-grade investments, comprehensive portfolio management tools, and educational resources to help investors make informed decisions.

How does TAAGC Global work?

Investors create an account, complete verification, and can browse investment opportunities from our partner companies. Once you find a suitable investment, you can invest directly through our platform. We handle all the paperwork and provide regular updates on your investments.

Is TAAGC Global regulated?

Yes, TAAGC Global operates in compliance with international financial regulations. We work with licensed partners and follow strict compliance procedures to ensure all investments meet regulatory requirements.

Investing

What is the minimum investment amount?

The minimum investment amount varies by project, typically ranging from $1,000 to $25,000. Each investment opportunity clearly states its minimum investment requirement.

How do I receive returns?

Returns are distributed directly to your TAAGC wallet based on the project's payout schedule (monthly, quarterly, or at maturity). You can then withdraw funds to your bank account or reinvest in new opportunities.

Can I withdraw early?

Early withdrawal options depend on the specific investment. Some projects allow early exit with penalties, while others are locked until maturity. Check the investment terms before investing.

How are investments selected?

Our investment team conducts thorough due diligence on all opportunities. We evaluate the project's fundamentals, management team, market potential, and risk factors before listing them on our platform.

Account Management

How do I create an account?

Click the "Register" button and fill in your details. You'll need to provide your name, email, and create a password. After registration, you'll need to verify your email and complete identity verification.

What documents are needed for verification?

You'll need a government-issued ID (passport, driver's license), proof of address (utility bill, bank statement), and a selfie for identity verification. All documents must be current and legible.

How long does verification take?

Verification typically takes 24-48 hours. In some cases, it may take up to 3 business days. You'll receive an email notification once your account is verified.

Can I have multiple accounts?

No, each user is limited to one account. Multiple accounts are not permitted and may result in suspension of all associated accounts.

Security

How secure is my information?

We use bank-level encryption and security measures to protect your data. All information is stored securely, and we never share your personal information with third parties without your consent.

Do you offer two-factor authentication?

Yes, we strongly recommend enabling two-factor authentication (2FA) for your account. You can enable it in your account security settings.

What if I forget my password?

Click the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. You'll receive an email with instructions to reset your password. Make sure to choose a strong, unique password.

Payments & Fees

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept bank transfers, credit/debit cards, and select cryptocurrencies. Payment methods may vary by region and investment type.

Are there any fees?

We charge a small platform fee on investments (typically 1-2%). There are no fees for deposits or withdrawals. Some payment processors may charge their own fees.

How long do withdrawals take?

Withdrawals typically process within 1-3 business days. Bank transfers may take additional time depending on your bank.

What currencies do you support?

We support USD, EUR, GBP, and select other major currencies. Cryptocurrency investments are available in Bitcoin, Ethereum, and USDT.
